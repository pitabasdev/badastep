B.Ed: Course, Full Form, Admission 2024, Colleges, Fees, Syllabus, Exam Dates, Jobs

Ed (Bachelor of Education) degree is a 2-year professional program for individuals aspiring to be teachers in schools, colleges, and other academic institutions. B Ed course is crucial for those who want to pursue a career in teaching and is a common qualification for teaching positions. B Ed curriculum majorly deals with educational psychology, teaching methodologies, curriculum development, educational technology, and classroom management. 

B.Ed Admission takes place both through merit and entrance exams, mostly BEd State Exams. Each state's admission focuses on training teachers for their states. Hence, eligibility criteria for B.Ed differs from one state to another since the admission process is also unique in every state of India. Although students from all over India can apply for any state B Ed Admission process, it must be kept in mind that there are various reservation benefits enjoyed by students from that particular state.

B Ed Admission Process

B.Ed Admission takes place through both Exams-based and Merit-based processes. Merit-based admission takes place when there are no entrance exams, and students get admission based on their marks in graduation.

Almost every state conducts its own entrance exams to admit students to the Bachelor of Education program. This helps states maintain a good number of teachers to teach in the schools.

B Ed Eligibility Criteria

Different colleges or states have different sets of requirements or eligibility criteria to apply for a BEd course; however, the general BEd eligibility criteria are mentioned below:

  • The candidate must be a graduate.
  • He/she should secure more than 50% marks in the final graduation exam.
  • Candidates belonging to reserved categories (SC/ST/OBC/PWD) enjoy a 5% relaxation in the minimum aggregate score required.
  • Few colleges have a minimum age limit of 19 years.

B.Ed Distance Education

The Distance B.Ed admission is done both based on merit list or entrance examination. The entrance examination for distance B.Ed courses is taken by the respective universities. For example, IGNOU, which is one of the most popular universities to pursue the distance B.Ed program conducts its entrance exam. Other popular universities such as Netaji Subhas Open University, and Lovely Professional University offer admission based on merit lists.

The following are the general eligibility details that an individual must keep in mind for the Distance B.Ed course

  • The candidates must obtain a minimum score of 50% in their graduation or equivalent examination. 
  • In the case of a B.Tech degree, the candidate must obtain a minimum score of 55% marks
  • The candidates of the reserved categories are given a relaxation of 5%.
  • The candidates must have trained in-service teachers. Candidates having at least two years of teaching experience, either at the primary or secondary level can take up the distance education course.
  • There is no maximum or minimum age requirement for admission
  • The general Course fees for Distance B.Ed range between INR 40,000 to INR 110,000.
