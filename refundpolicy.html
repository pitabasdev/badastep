Refund Policy

1. Overview of the Refund Policy

Thank you for buying our courses. We ensure that our users have a rewarding experience while they discover, assess, and purchase our courses, whether it is instructor-led or self-paced training.

By purchasing a training course on badastep, you agree to our Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, and Refund Policy.

2. Cancellation & Refunds: Online Training

To request a refund, please raise the request before the commencement of the batch or within 7 days of enrollment, whichever is earlier. The money-back guarantee is void if:

  • The participant has accessed more than 25% of the content of any e-learning course.
  • The participant has attended Online Classrooms or received recordings for even 1 day.
  • The user downloads the E-Book for the course.

Refund requests beyond 7 days of purchasing the course or after course commencement will not be accepted. Also, any booking fee paid towards securing a seat is non-refundable.

3. Cancellation & Refunds for Events

badastep reserves the right to postpone or cancel an event or change the location due to insufficient enrollments, instructor illness, or force majeure events (like floods, earthquakes, political instability, etc).

If badastep cancels an event:

  • 100% of course fees will be refunded if the refund request is raised within 10 days of purchase.
  • Travel, logistics, or any personal expenses incurred will not be refunded.

If an event is canceled or postponed, participants will be rescheduled to an upcoming batch without extra charges.

4. Refund Request Process

Refund requests can be initiated by emailing with proper reasoning at refunds@badastep.in.

For duplicate payments, refunds will be processed via the original payment method within 10 working days after notification by the customer.

All refunds will be processed within 30 working days after approval of the refund request.

badastep reserves the right to revise the terms & conditions of this policy without prior notice.

For any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at support@badastep.in.
