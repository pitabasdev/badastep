Bachelor of Technology (BTech)

Bachelor of Technology (BTech) is a professional undergraduate engineering degree for candidates who have completed four years of study in an accredited university or college. Every year, India graduates more than 10 lakh engineers, and there are over 2500 engineering colleges and 1300 polytechnic institutes in the country.

Admission Process

The JEE Main and JEE Advanced entrance exams are the most common for BTech admission. In addition to these entrance exams at the national level, students can apply to the course by taking a variety of entrance exams at the state and private levels.

Eligibility

Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics is the minimum requirement for admission to a BTech program. A BTech degree focuses on the development of diverse technical skills and knowledge connected to the utilization of engineering concepts.

BTech Admission Process

The colleges and different entrance exams play a significant role in the BTech admissions process. The BTech admission process is as follows:

  • Students interested in applying to the BTech degree program must take a variety of admission exams or tests, including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, WBJEE, and AP EAMCET.
  • The institutes use these entrance exams to assist them in enrolling students based on merit.

BTech Eligibility

The students who study Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as their main subjects are eligible for BTech Qualification. Below are the eligibility criteria:

  • A student with a diploma in engineering domains is eligible to enroll in a Bachelor of Technology programme.
  • Students must qualify for their 10+2 examination from the science stream from any board.
  • Students must have secured 60% marks in their class 12.
  • Eligibility criteria can vary from institute to institute.

Top BTech Entrance Examination

Several institutions offer several exams to gain admission into BTech courses:

  • JEE Mains: To select candidates for admission to B.Tech. programs at NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs.
  • JEE Advanced: The screening test for applicants to the IITs.
  • WBJEE: A state-level entrance examination for engineering courses in West Bengal.
  • BITSAT: For students interested in enrolling in BTech programs at BITS Pilani.
  • VITEEE: A university-level entrance test for VIT BTech programs.

BTech Placements

In every industry, there is an excellent need for BTech graduates. Every organization, large or small, public or private, hires engineers in various positions. BTech graduates are hired in various industries, including KPO/Research Analytics, Accounting, and Finance. Recruiters from top companies visit several institutions throughout the last year to find suitable candidates.
