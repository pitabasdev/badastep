Asked Questions

At badastep, we strive to provide a seamless experience for students and educators. Below, you’ll find answers to frequently asked questions that can assist you in navigating through our platform's features and services.

Our platform is designed to simplify the process of finding and applying to various educational institutes, courses, and programs. If you have any queries related to admissions, payments, or managing your account, please refer to the FAQs below.

Why won’t my payment go through?
Payments may not go through for several reasons, such as incorrect card details, insufficient funds, or an expired card. Ensure that all your payment information is correct, or try using a different payment method. If the issue persists, please contact our support team for further assistance.
How do I get a refund?
If you wish to request a refund, please reach out to our support team within 7 days of your payment. Ensure that you provide your transaction ID and the reason for the refund. Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days depending on your bank or payment provider.
How do I redeem a coupon?
To redeem a coupon, go to the payment page, and enter your coupon code in the designated field before completing your transaction. If the coupon is valid, the discount will be applied automatically. Please note that some coupons may have specific conditions such as expiration dates or course restrictions.
Why aren’t my courses showing in my account?
If your enrolled courses are not showing in your account, try refreshing the page or logging out and logging back in. Ensure you are using the correct email ID associated with your account. If the issue continues, please contact our support team for further assistance.
How do I update my account details?
To update your account details, log in to your profile and navigate to the "Account Settings" section. From there, you can edit your personal information, change your password, or update your contact details. If you encounter any difficulties, reach out to our support team for guidance.

For any additional queries or support requests, feel free to get in touch with us through the contact form on our website. We are here to help you make the most of your learning journey at badastep.

If you need further assistance or have suggestions, please email us at support@badastep.in. We value your feedback and are constantly striving to improve our services for a better user experience.
