Privacy Policy

1. Overview of the Privacy Policy

WET PEDIA FOUNDATION is the manager, moderator, and operator of the website www.badastep.in and all of its versions including mobile and applications. This privacy policy document clarifies how we use the data and information collected from users. The policy is prepared solely for badastep and not for the websites linked through it. Please refer to respective documents for more details.

2. Privacy of Users Below 14 Years of Age

The website is not designed to attract users below 14 years of age, and we do not intend to retain data of such users. If we identify any such data, it will be deleted promptly.

3. Cookies and IP Tracking Policy

The website stores non-personal identifiable data, such as IP addresses and cookies, which include search history, queries submitted, education interests, date and time, domain, and advertisement response. We may also use a conversion pixel (a 1px x 1px image) to track user behavior on our leads collection form as part of our business operations.

4. Information Collected by the Website

The website collects and stores data submitted by users. As we allow users to request information from third-party sources listed on the website, we share the data with the respective institutes to fulfill the request. The privacy of such data is governed by the mutual agreement between the sources and users.

  • Contact Forms: The contact forms collect user interests, educational qualifications, and age to simplify the data presentation process.
  • Login/Sign-up: By creating a user account, visitors agree to the terms and conditions of the website. We may use the data collected for trend analysis and may share it with educational service providers depending on the usage history. Users also agree to receive SMS and emails containing information related to the courses or exams they have shown interest in.
  • Testimonials: Users providing testimonials consent to their public display, which may also be shared with our clients.
  • Forums: Comments posted in forums and our comment sections become the sole property of Prepp.in, as they provide valuable insights into the consumer thought process.
5. Opt-In Mails and Other Uses of Data

The company may use data collected through the website in various ways:

  • Opt-In Mails: We send customized emails to users who opt-in for newsletters or notifications on admission processes and entrance tests. Users can unsubscribe from the service at any time.
  • Feedback: The company may contact its visitors or users for feedback on any new features or services.
  • Geographical Data: We use geographical or demographical data to provide location-wise customized results.
  • Cookies and IP Data: These are used to provide the most relevant results through search functionality.

For any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at support@badastep.in.
